ActBlue Session Keepalive

A small, open-source tool that keeps an active ActBlue session from idling out.

Unofficial · not affiliated with ActBlue

What it does

While turned on, it sends one ordinary signed-in request to secure.actblue.com every few minutes so your session does not go idle. It uses the login your browser already has.

You choose how long to keep your session warm, from 30 minutes up to Unlimited (default 3 hours). After that window it pauses and lets the session idle out on its own. "Check now" extends the window.

Your privacy

  • No password and no session token is ever read, stored, or sent. Your browser holds your login.
  • No analytics, no telemetry, no tracking, no server. The only request it makes is to ActBlue.
  • Your settings and last status are kept in local browser storage and do not sync anywhere.

Good to know

  • Keeping a session open longer is convenient, so use it on a device you control. Lock your screen when you step away, and sign out when you are done.
  • It is a load-unpacked extension, so the code you load is what runs. The source is open; you can read it.
  • Provided as is, with no warranty. ActBlue can change its site at any time, which may stop this from working. Use it at your own discretion.
